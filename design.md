# biologicalcontrol.org — design system

Locked visual system for the IITA Biological Control Folklore archive.

## Brand

- **Domain:** biologicalcontrol.org
- **Concept:** folklore archive for everyone who was there — one community, shared compound life
- **Identity:** Cursor product UI — sharp Geist sans, warm beige paper, high contrast, packs a punch
- **Person cards:** IITA-era business-card placeholders (cream stock, hairline rules, mono inventory codes)

## Genre & structure

- **Genre:** modern-minimal (Cursor school)
- **Home macrostructure:** Ecosystem Index (featured / people / galleries rails)
- **Story pages:** Long Document in Geist Sans (no serif, no drop cap)
- **Nav:** N9 Edge-aligned minimal (domain wordmark left · links + CTA right)
- **Footer:** Ft2 Inline single line + short statement
- **Joke themes** (Typewriter / 1994): opt-in via Settings only — not part of the design system

## Type rules

- Sans-only · bold display · tight tracking · base ≥ 18px
- Solid ink primary CTAs · outlined secondary · no purple / no serif magazine vibe

## Voice

- One community of people who were there — not staff vs family product lanes
- Wonderful, funny, unforgettable compound stories; science is context, not the victory lap
- World Food Prize without Nobel lectures
- CTAs like “Share a story from the compound” — warm, not corporate
