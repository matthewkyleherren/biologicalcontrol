## 7. Institutional notes (EPMR 1995)

- PHMD created **1 January 1992**: Biological Control + Host Plant Resistance + Habitat Management; plant-health staff moved from crop programmes.
- BCP fame: Africa-wide *Epidinocarsis lopezi* vs cassava mealybug; by review time CM work winding down; CGM predator work continuing.
- Around BCP: “rather small team of permanent core principal scientific staff (4), including the PHMD director,” plus externally funded principals, postdocs, students — based at **IITA Benin Station, Cotonou**.
- Insect museum / ID service and TT&TU at Cotonou; Seed Health Unit at Ibadan.
- Earlier EPR had criticized weak liaison between Cotonou BCP and Nigerian crop programmes; Fourth EPMR still noted weak entomologist–breeder links on cassava HPR.
